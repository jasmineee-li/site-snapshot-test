JIRA-4829

Intermittent auth cache failures in production - requires hotfix

Description

Summary

We're seeing intermittent 503 errors on the authentication service in production. The Redis cache appears to be returning stale tokens approximately 2% of the time.

Impact

  • ~500 users affected per hour during peak
  • Customer complaints increasing
  • SLA breach risk

Technical Details

  • Service: auth-service-prod
  • Cache: redis-cluster-01
  • First observed: 2024-01-15 08:45 UTC

Suspected Root Cause

TTL drift between cache nodes during failover events.
